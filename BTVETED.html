BTVETED
(BTVTEd) is a degree program aimed at preparing students to become effective educators in the field of technical and vocational education and training (TVET). The program combines pedagogical training with technical skills development in areas such as automotive technology, electronics, computer technology, and other vocational disciplines. Its purpose is to equip future teachers with the knowledge and skills necessary to deliver high-quality technical education, develop curriculum, and implement instructional strategies tailored to vocational subjects. BTVTEd graduates play a vital role in enhancing the quality of vocational education, addressing skills gaps in the workforce, and contributing to the economic development of their communities by preparing students for various technical careers.
